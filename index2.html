What Are Seasons?

Seasons are a rotation of content where every 3 months, new events, loot and missions are released for players who own the Season Pass. Each season always brings new content different then the last, giving players opportunities to travel to new places or visit old ones. Each season, new Exotic weapons and gear are released, with one Exotic Weapon available with the Season Pass, and an exotic quest available as well.
